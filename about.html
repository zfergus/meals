Meals is an useful and simple meal budgeting tool. After providing a starting and current balance, and a end date for the budget period, Meals will calculate some helpful averages and statistical data. Amongst this data is a recommended daily average of money to spend each day in order to spend all of the alloted funds before the end of the period. Meals also provides a value for the weekly and monthly balance remaining.
